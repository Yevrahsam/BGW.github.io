BGW Discord Server

BGW is all about creating a friendly non-toxic community, where you can express your great personality as much as you like! We have lots of channels for your interests, however we're always listening to suggestions from the community.

BGW Ban Appeals

If you have been banned from BGW and feel it was unfair or you deserve another chance, please complete the google form below. Make sure that you are 100% truthful as we will fact check your appeal. Remember you are trying to convince us to unban you.

If you do not hear back from us within a week we may not have been able to contact you so try to join the server to see the result.
